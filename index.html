Biography

Pengcheng Xie works for Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory in the United States as a postdoctoral scholar working with Prof. Stefan Wild, specializing in computational mathematics, mathematical optimization, machine learning, and numerical analysis. He holds a PhD from the Chinese Academy of Sciences advised by Prof. Ya-xiang Yuan. Pengcheng has published in major journals and presented at SIAM NCC 2024, ISMP 2024, SIAM NCC2024, ICIAM 2023, and CSIAM 2022.
